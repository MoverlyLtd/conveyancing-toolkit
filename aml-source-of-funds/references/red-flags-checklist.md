# AML Red Flags Checklist — UK Conveyancing

Comprehensive red flags for source of funds in residential property
transactions. When any flag is triggered, document it, risk-assess, and
escalate to MLRO/compliance officer as appropriate.

## Client Behaviour Red Flags

| # | Red Flag | Risk Level | Response |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Reluctance to provide source of funds documentation | High | Do not proceed without documentation. Consider SAR. |
| 2 | Providing inconsistent or contradictory information about funding | High | Investigate discrepancies. Escalate to MLRO. |
| 3 | Client acting on behalf of an undisclosed third party | High | Establish beneficial owner identity. Consider SAR. |
| 4 | Unusual pressure to complete quickly without clear reason | Medium | Investigate reason for urgency. Document assessment. |
| 5 | Client is evasive about their occupation or source of wealth | High | Cannot proceed without satisfactory explanation. |
| 6 | Client does not want to be seen at the property or avoids inspection | Medium | Investigate. May indicate no genuine interest in property. |
| 7 | Client has no connection to the area of purchase | Low-Med | Legitimate reasons exist, but explore and document. |
| 8 | Use of intermediaries or agents with no clear need | Medium | Establish why agent is involved. Verify identity of all parties. |

## Funding Structure Red Flags

| # | Red Flag | Risk Level | Response |
|---|---|---|---|
| 9 | Last-minute change of funding source | High | Re-do source of funds checks on new element. Document reason for change. Escalate. |
| 10 | Third-party funding with no clear family or commercial relationship | High | Full CDD on third party. Understand and document purpose. Consider SAR. |
| 11 | Cash purchase (no mortgage) | Medium-High | Removes lender as additional CDD layer. Apply EDD to all funding. |
| 12 | Cash-heavy deposits — large cash amounts paid into bank accounts | High | Trace origin of cash. Amounts >€10,000 may trigger bank reporting. |
| 13 | Funds routed through multiple accounts or intermediaries | High | Trace full chain. Understand each intermediary. Document. |
| 14 | Funds from multiple unrelated sources for a single purchase | Medium | Verify each source independently. Understand relationships. |
| 15 | Deposits paid directly to estate agent rather than through solicitor | Medium | Recover audit trail. Verify source of those specific funds. |
| 16 | Round-sum transfers with no obvious connection to transaction amounts | Medium | Investigate why amounts don't correspond to expected figures. |

## Transaction Red Flags

| # | Red Flag | Risk Level | Response |
|---|---|---|---|
| 17 | Purchase price significantly above or below market value | High | Understand commercial rationale. Overpayment may be laundering vehicle. |
| 18 | Multiple property purchases in a short period | High | Understand investment rationale. Full CDD on funding for each. |
| 19 | Rapid buy-sell (property purchased and resold quickly) | High | May indicate property flipping for laundering. Investigate. |
| 20 | Unusual legal structures (offshore companies, trusts) with no clear purpose | High | Full CDD on structure. Identify UBOs. Apply EDD. |
| 21 | Request to receive funds back (e.g. aborted transaction) to a different account | High | Funds should return to originating account only. |
| 22 | Request to send completion funds to unexpected third-party account | High | Verify with seller's solicitor. May indicate fraud or layering. |
| 23 | Transaction involves property in a high-risk location with no client connection | Medium | Document rationale. Consider broader risk profile. |
| 24 | Buyer and seller appear connected or related but have not disclosed this | High | Investigate relationship. May indicate circular transactions. |

## Overseas / Cross-Border Red Flags

| # | Red Flag | Risk Level | Response |
|---|---|---|---|
| 25 | Funds from high-risk jurisdiction (FATF grey/black list) | High | Apply EDD. Source country risk assessment mandatory. Consider SAR. |
| 26 | Funds routed through countries with no connection to client | High | Trace full route. Understand reason for each jurisdiction. |
| 27 | Use of correspondent banking chains making origin unclear | High | Request SWIFT confirmations for full chain. |
| 28 | Client is non-resident purchasing UK property | Medium | Legitimate but apply appropriate due diligence. Check overseas entities register if applicable. |
| 29 | Funds converted through multiple currencies | Medium-High | Currency conversion evidence for each step. |
| 30 | Power of attorney used by overseas client | Medium | Verify PoA, verify identity of attorney and donor. |

## Digital Asset / Crypto Red Flags

| # | Red Flag | Risk Level | Response |
|---|---|---|---|
| 31 | Funds derived from cryptocurrency with no auditable trail | High | Must establish original acquisition method and trace to fiat. |
| 32 | Crypto exchanged on unregulated or non-KYC platform | High | Cannot verify identity of counterparty. Apply EDD. Consider refusing. |
| 33 | Multiple crypto-to-crypto conversions before fiat conversion | High | Potential layering. Full transaction history required. |
| 34 | Privacy coins (Monero, Zcash) in the transaction chain | High | Designed to be untraceable. Significant AML risk. Escalate. |
| 35 | Large crypto gains with no evidence of original investment | High | May indicate proceeds of crime or unreported income. |

## Gift-Specific Red Flags

| # | Red Flag | Risk Level | Response |
|---|---|---|---|
| 36 | Gift from person with no apparent relationship to buyer | High | Full CDD on giftor. Understand and document rationale. |
| 37 | Giftor unable or unwilling to provide ID or bank statements | High | Cannot accept gift without giftor CDD. |
| 38 | Gift amount is disproportionate to giftor's apparent means | High | Source of wealth check on giftor required. |
| 39 | "Gift" that is actually a loan (obligation to repay) | Medium-High | Changes AML and lender disclosure requirements. Clarify in writing. |
| 40 | Gift from overseas with no clear connection to client | High | Apply overseas + gift checks combined. EDD. |

## Response Actions Summary

- **Document** — record every red flag observed and your assessment, even
  if you conclude it is benign
- **Investigate** — gather additional information before making a decision
- **Escalate** — report to MLRO/compliance officer for review
- **SAR** — if suspicion remains after investigation, file SAR with NCA
  through MLRO
- **DAML** — if you need to continue acting while suspicious, request
  consent from NCA
- **Decline** — you can decline to act if the risk cannot be mitigated.
  Be careful not to tip off when doing so.
- **Record** — whatever you decide, record your reasoning. The SRA/CLC
  will want to see your thought process, not just the outcome.
