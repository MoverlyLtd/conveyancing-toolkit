`/propertyPack/otherIssues/excessiveNoise`

This path handles whether there is excessive noise from roads, railways, aircraft, industry, or other sources that might affect the property. The schema includes:

- `/propertyPack/otherIssues/excessiveNoise/attachments` for uploading noise-related documentation (sound surveys, complaints, correspondence, etc.)
- Boolean field to indicate if excessive noise affects the property
- Text field for details about the noise source and impact

This is part of the broader "Other Issues" section that covers environmental and neighborhood factors that could affect the property's desirability or value.
