`/propertyPack/environmentalIssues/contaminatedLand`

This path covers contaminated land concerns, including whether the property is on potentially contaminated land and any remediation that may have been carried out.

If you have documents related to contaminated land (such as environmental reports, remediation certificates, or contamination assessments), they would go to:
`/propertyPack/environmentalIssues/contaminatedLand/attachments`
