`/propertyPack/environmentalIssues/groundStability`

This path covers ground stability concerns including subsidence risk, ground movement, unstable land, mining subsidence, and related geological issues that could affect the property's foundation or structural integrity.

If you have a ground stability report or survey, the attachments would go to:
`/propertyPack/environmentalIssues/groundStability/attachments`

Note: This is different from `/propertyPack/specialistIssues/subsidenceOrStructuralFault` which covers actual subsidence damage that has already occurred, rather than environmental ground stability risks.
