`/propertyPack/listingAndConservation/hasTreePreservationOrder`

This is the PDTF path for tree preservation orders. The schema includes sub-paths for whether work has been carried out under the TPO - if there are documents related to TPO work, they would go to:

`/propertyPack/listingAndConservation/hasTreePreservationOrder/workCarriedOut/attachments`
