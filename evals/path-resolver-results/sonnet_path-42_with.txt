Based on the PDTF schema, this would be recorded at:

**`/propertyPack/alterationsAndChanges/nonResidentialPurposes`**

This path captures when part of a residential property is used for non-residential purposes, which includes home office use. The schema asks whether any part of the property is used for purposes other than as a single private dwelling house.

If there are any documents related to the home office use (such as planning permission for change of use, business rates correspondence, or local authority permissions), they would go to:

**`/propertyPack/alterationsAndChanges/nonResidentialPurposes/attachments`**

Note: This is different from the general alterations paths - `nonResidentialPurposes` has a top-level `attachments` property rather than the separate planning/building regs structure used by physical alterations.
