## 1.14 May your firm act if the person dealing with the transaction or a member of his immediate family is the seller?

Not when the person dealing is the seller or a member of the seller’s immediate family. Your firm may act if a separate fee earner of no less standing or partner in the firm acts for us and no conflict of interest exists.

## 1.15 May your firm act if the person dealing with the transaction or a member of his immediate family is the borrower?

Not when the person dealing is the seller or a member of the borrower’s immediate family. Your firm may act if a separate fee earner of no less standing or partner in the firm acts for us and no conflict of interest exists.

## 3.1.5 What other documents are acceptable for verifying identity?

List A:
Current signed Non UK/EEA passport – must be accompanied with evidence of permanent right to reside in the UK.
Current EEA National Identity Card.
Current signed UK Travel Document (looks like a UK passport but states 'Titre de voyage').
Current Full UK Driving License (old paper style).
Latest HMRC Tax Code Notification (states tax code for the forthcoming tax year) which states full name.
State Pension or Benefits Book/Notification Letter which states full name.
List B:
HMRC Revenue Tax Code Notification.
State Pension or Benefits Book/Notification.
Letter-dated within the current tax year.
Current Tax Awards Letter- dated within the current tax year.
Current local rent card or tenancy agreement.
Items shown in both lists can only be used for one option not both.
