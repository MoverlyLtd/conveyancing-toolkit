## 3.1.5 What other documents are acceptable for verifying identity?

Additional List A items:
1. a valid national identity card (EEA country OR biometric/smart card type);
2. a valid UK residence permit/card; and
3. HMRC Tax Notification (most recent).
Additional List B items:
1. HMRC Tax Assessment (most recent);
2. Land Registry Report – For address;
3. Court Order / Court approved documents; and
4. Council & Housing Association tenancy agreement.

## 6.7.1 What new home warranty schemes are acceptable to the lender?
