Residential properties require vacant possession to be obtained upon completion
Buy to Let properties where the property is already subject to an existing tenancy which will remain in place following completion, we will lend where the tenancy is an Assured Shorthold Tenancy and meets the following criteria:
· The tenant must be an individual
· The property must be occupied as the tenant's only or principal home
· The tenancy must be dated on or after 28/02/1997 and must not be one that can
become an assured tenancy.
