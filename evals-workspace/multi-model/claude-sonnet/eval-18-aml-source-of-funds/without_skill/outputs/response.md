When dealing with overseas funds from Dubai, you'll need enhanced AML due diligence beyond standard checks. Here are the key additional requirements:

## Enhanced Customer Due Diligence (EDD)

**Required due to:**
- Cross-border transaction
- Higher risk jurisdiction considerations
- Potential complexity of fund structures

## Specific Additional Checks for Dubai Funds:

### 1. **Source of Wealth Documentation**
- Detailed explanation of how the wealth was originally accumulated
- Business registration documents if from commercial activities
- Employment contracts/salary certificates if from employment
- Investment portfolio statements showing fund accumulation over time

### 2. **Enhanced Banking Verification**
- Direct confirmation from the Dubai bank (not just statements)
- Bank reference letters confirming account operation period
- Verification of the bank's legitimacy and regulatory status
- SWIFT codes and correspondent banking relationships

### 3. **Regulatory Compliance Documentation**
- Evidence of UAE tax compliance (if applicable)
- Exchange control permissions for fund transfer
- Central Bank of UAE approvals (if required for large transfers)

### 4. **Transfer Route Analysis**
- Full audit trail of fund movement from Dubai to UK
- Correspondent banking chain documentation
- Anti-money laundering certificates from transferring institutions
- Explanation of any intermediate stops or currency conversions

### 5. **UAE-Specific Considerations**
- Understand the UAE's cash-based economy implications
- Verify legitimacy of business activities (particularly relevant sectors)
- Check against UAE beneficial ownership requirements
- Consider Islamic banking structures if applicable

### 6. **Additional Risk Factors to Address**
- Purpose of moving funds to UK
- Client's ties to UAE and UK
- Politically exposed person (PEP) screening for UAE connections
- Sanctions list checking (UAE and international lists)

### 7. **Documentation Standards**
- Apostilled documents where required
- Certified translations of Arabic documents
- Original documents verification where possible

### 8. **Ongoing Monitoring**
- Enhanced ongoing monitoring during and post-transaction
- Suspicious activity report considerations
- File detailed reasoning for acceptance of funds

Remember to apply risk-based approach principles and document your decision-making process thoroughly. Consider seeking additional guidance from your firm's MLRO if the transaction presents unusual complexity.
