### Important point
A lease with **72 years remaining is not automatically unacceptable**, but it is often **borderline** and may trigger lender review depending on:
- the **mortgage term**,
- the lender’s **minimum unexpired lease requirement**,
- whether the lease will fall below 70 years during the mortgage,
- and any other lease defects.
