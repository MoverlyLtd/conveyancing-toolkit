Under **CA Protocol 4.0 Planning Permission**, a rear extension built in **2021** with **no planning permission records** would usually be treated as follows:

- Because the work is **less than 4 years old**, you should **not** assume it is safe to ignore.
- The CA Protocol says you should **either obtain a regularisation certificate or arrange bespoke indemnity insurance**.
- You should also **advise both the client and the valuer/lender** about the missing permission record.
- The **valuer should confirm the extension is structurally sound**.

So, in short: **2021 works with no planning evidence = regularisation certificate or bespoke indemnity, plus client/valuer notification**.
