### SDLT for a first-time buyer at £510,000 (England/Northern Ireland)

First-time buyer relief (current rules) means:
- **0%** on the first **£425,000**
- **5%** on the portion from **£425,001 to £625,000**
- Relief is only available if the purchase price is **£625,000 or less** (which £510,000 is)

#### Calculation
- 0% on £425,000 = **£0**
- 5% on £510,000 − £425,000 = 5% of **£85,000** = **£4,250**

✅ **Total SDLT payable: £4,250**
