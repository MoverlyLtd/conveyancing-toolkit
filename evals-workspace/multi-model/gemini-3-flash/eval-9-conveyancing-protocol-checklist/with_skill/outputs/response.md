To give you the most accurate checklist, are you acting for the **buyer** or the **seller**?

Since you are at the point of exchange, you need to verify that all **Stage B (Pre-Exchange)** requirements are satisfied and prepare for **Stage C (Exchange)**. 

Below are the key items from the Law Society Conveyancing Protocol (2019) that must be checked immediately prior to and during exchange.

### For the Buyer's Conveyancer

| # | Item | Protocol Ref |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **B3** | Ensure all necessary enquiries have been raised and satisfactory replies received | Stage B, para 3 |
| **B4** | Provide a comprehensive report on title to the client | Stage B, para 4 |
| **B5** | Report to the lender (if applicable) and request mortgage funds | Stage B, para 5 |
| **B8** | Confirm you have the signed contract in your possession | Stage B, para 8 |
| **B9** | Confirm cleared deposit funds are in your client account | Stage B, para 9 |
| **B11** | Verify all search results are satisfactory and issues resolved | Stage B, para 11 |
| **B12** | Confirm all mortgage offer conditions have been met | Stage B, para 12 |
| **C1** | Exchange contracts using the agreed Law Society formula (e.g., Formula B) | Stage C, para 1 |
| **C2** | Notify the client immediately that exchange has happened | Stage C, para 2 |
| **C3** | Send the deposit to the seller’s conveyancer (or confirm held as stakeholder) | Stage C, para 3 |
| **C8** | Remind the client to put buildings insurance in place from the moment of exchange | Stage C, para 8 |

### For the Seller's Conveyancer

| # | Item | Protocol Ref |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **B6** | Ensure all enquiries have been answered fully and promptly | Stage B, para 6 |
| **B7** | Ensure the contract and completion date are agreed by all parties | Stage B, para 7 |
| **B8** | Confirm you have the signed contract in your possession | Stage B, para 8 |
| **B10** | Confirm the fixtures and fittings list (TA10) is agreed and final | Stage B, para 10 |
| **C1** | Exchange contracts using the agreed Law Society formula | Stage C, para 1 |
| **C2** | Notify the client immediately that exchange has happened | Stage C, para 2 |
| **C4** | Notify the estate agent that exchange has taken place | Stage C, para 4 |

### General Obligations to Remember
*   **Formulae:** Ensure you use the standard Law Society formulae for exchanging contracts by telephone (General Obligation, para e).
*   **Standard Conditions:** Only make essential amendments to the Standard Conditions of Sale (General Obligation, para f).
*   **Client Interests:** Remember that your client's best interests always override protocol obligations.
