Stamp Duty Land Tax (SDLT) for First-Time Buyers in England and Northern Ireland:

- The first £300,000 of the property value is exempt from SDLT for first-time buyers.
- On the remaining £210,000 (£510,000 - £300,000), the SDLT rate is 5%.

Calculation:
- £0 on the first £300,000 (as this is exempt for first-time buyers)
- 5% on the remaining £210,000 = £10,500

Therefore, the total stamp duty payable as a first-time buyer for a £510,000 property would be £10,500.

It's important to note that this calculation is based on the current stamp duty rates in England and Northern Ireland. The stamp duty rules and rates may be different in Scotland and Wales, so if the property is located in those regions, the calculation would need to be adjusted accordingly.
