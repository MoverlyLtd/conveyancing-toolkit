1. £200,000 from Savings:

   - Proof of the origin of the savings, such as payslips, tax returns, or other documentation showing the client's income sources.

2. £100,000 Gift from Parents:
   - A letter from the parents confirming the gift, including their full name, address, and contact details.
   - Proof of the parents' identity and address, such as a copy of their passport or driver's license and a utility bill or bank statement.
   - Bank statements or other evidence showing the transfer of the gift funds from the parents' account to the client's account.

3. £450,000 Mortgage:
   - A copy of the mortgage offer or agreement, clearly showing the amount of the mortgage and the lender's details.
   - Proof of the client's identity and address, such as a copy of their passport or driver's license and a utility bill or bank statement.
   - Evidence of the client's income and ability to afford the mortgage, such as payslips, tax returns, or a self-employment income statement.

- Conduct a Customer Due Diligence (CDD) check on the client to verify their identity and address.
