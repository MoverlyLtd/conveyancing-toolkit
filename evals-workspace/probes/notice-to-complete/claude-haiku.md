Under the Standard Conditions of Sale (5th Edition), the seller can serve a notice to complete if the buyer fails to complete on the contractual completion date of Friday, 4 April 2025.

The relevant sections are:

Section 6.8 - Notice to Complete
This section states that if the buyer fails to complete the purchase on the contractual completion date, the seller may serve a notice to complete on the buyer.

Section 6.8.1 states that the notice must specify a new completion date, which must be at least 10 working days after the date the notice is served.

Therefore, in this case, the seller can serve a notice to complete on the buyer at any time after Friday, 4 April 2025. The new completion date specified in the notice must be at least 10 working days after the date the notice is served.

For example, if the seller serves the notice on Monday, 7 April 2025, the new completion date specified in the notice must be no earlier than Friday, 18 April 2025 (10 working days after 7 April).

The notice to complete will then expire on the new completion date specified in the notice. In the example above, the notice would expire on Friday, 18 April 2025.

If the buyer still fails to complete by the new completion date, the seller may then have grounds to rescind the contract and claim compensation from the buyer.
