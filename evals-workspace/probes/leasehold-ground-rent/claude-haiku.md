The Leasehold Reform (Ground Rent) Act 2022, which came into force on 30 June 2022, aims to address the issue of escalating ground rents in new residential leases.

- For new residential leases granted on or after 30 June 2022, ground rents are capped at a maximum of £0 per year (effectively prohibiting ground rents).
- The Act applies to leases of houses and flats in England and Wales, with some limited exceptions.
- Existing leases granted before 30 June 2022 are not affected by this Act.
