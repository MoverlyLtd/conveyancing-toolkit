For a property purchase of £350,000 using the online portal, the current Land Registry fees are:

**Registration fee: £270**

This applies to the value band of £200,001 to £500,000. The online portal offers a discount compared to postal applications - the postal fee for the same value would be £540, so you're saving £270 by applying online.

**Additional considerations:**

- This covers the standard transfer registration (Form TR1)
- If you're also registering a new mortgage, there would be an additional fee of £40 online (£80 by post) for each charge
- The fee is payable when you submit the application online
- Processing times are typically faster online (usually 4-6 weeks vs 8-12 weeks for postal applications)

**Payment methods:**
The online portal accepts debit cards, credit cards, and CHAPS payments.

Make sure you have all required documents ready before starting your online application, including the completed TR1 form, mortgage deed (if applicable), and any supporting evidence such as identity documents.

These fees are current as of 2024, but it's worth double-checking on the Land Registry website (gov.uk) when you're ready to submit your application, as fees can change.
