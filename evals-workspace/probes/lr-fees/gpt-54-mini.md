For a **£350,000 purchase**, the **HM Land Registry fee for registering the transfer** using the **online portal (electronic/AP1 via portal)** is currently:

### **£135**

That is the fee under the **current Land Registry scale for electronic applications** for a transfer of whole where the consideration is **over £200,000 and up to £500,000**.

### Relevant fee scale
For **electronic** registration applications:
- **£100,001 to £200,000**: **£95**
- **Over £200,000 to £500,000**: **£135**
- **Over £500,000 to £1,000,000**: **£295**

Because your purchase price is **£350,000**, it falls in the **£200,001–£500,000** band.

### Practical point
If the application is submitted **on paper** instead of electronically, the fee is higher. For the same price band, the paper fee is **£270**.

### Legal basis
These are the fees under the **Land Registration Fee Order** and HM Land Registry’s current fee scale, applied by reference to the **consideration/value stated in the transfer**.
