The current Land Registry fees for registering the transfer of a property valued at £350,000 using the online portal are as follows:

1. Transfer of Whole Registration Fee:
   - The fee for a property valued at £350,000 is £270.
   - This fee is charged for the registration of the transfer of the whole of the registered title.

2. Electronic Document Registration Service (EDRS) Fee:
   - The EDRS fee for an application submitted online is £6.
   - This fee covers the cost of the electronic document submission and registration service.

Therefore, the total Land Registry fees for registering the transfer of a property valued at £350,000 using the online portal are:

Transfer of Whole Registration Fee: £270
EDRS Fee: £6
Total Land Registry Fees: £276

Please note that these fees are current as of the time of this response. It is always advisable to check the Land Registry website (www.gov.uk/guidance/hm-land-registry-registration-services-fees) for the most up-to-date fee information, as they are subject to change.
