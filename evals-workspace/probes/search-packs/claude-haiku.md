When buying a residential property in England, the standard and recommended searches that a buyer's conveyancer should order are:

1. Local Authority Search:
   - This search provides information about the property and the surrounding area, such as planning permissions, building regulations, road schemes, and environmental health issues.
   - Cost: Typically between £100 - £300, depending on the local authority.

2. Drainage and Water Search:
   - This search provides information about the water and sewerage services serving the property, including the location of public drains and water mains.
   - Cost: Typically between £50 - £100.

3. Environmental Search:
   - This search identifies potential environmental risks, such as contaminated land, flood risk, and mining/mineral extraction activities.
   - Cost: Typically between £50 - £150.

4. Energy and Infrastructure Search:
   - This search provides information about energy infrastructure, such as oil/gas pipelines, wind farms, and overhead power lines, which may affect the property.
   - Cost: Typically between £50 - £100.

5. Chancel Repair Liability Search:
   - This search checks whether the property is liable for chancel repair contributions, which are a historic obligation to contribute towards the upkeep of the local church.
   - Cost: Typically between £20 - £50.

6. Land Registry Official Copy of the Register and Title Plan:
   - This provides the official documentation of the property's legal title and any registered interests or charges on the property.
   - Cost: Typically between £3 - £6 per document.

In addition to the above standard searches, the following recommended searches may also be considered:

7. Coal Mining Search:
   - This search is recommended for properties located in areas with a history of coal mining activity, to identify any potential risks or liabilities.
   - Cost: Typically between £20 - £50.

8. Flood Risk Search:
   - This search provides detailed information about the property's flood risk, including the likelihood and potential impact of flooding.
   - Cost: Typically between £20 - £50.

The total cost of these searches can vary depending on the location and specific requirements of the property, but a rough estimate for the standard and recommended searches would be between £300 - £800.

It's important to note that the conveyancer may also recommend additional searches based on the specific circumstances of the property and the buyer's needs. The buyer should discuss the search requirements with their conveyancer to ensure that all necessary and appropriate searches are ordered.
